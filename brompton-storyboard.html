Test Ride
📍 Balfes Bike Shop, London
Andy spots a Brompton in the window and steps inside.
Andy
"What can you tell me about Brompton? I keep seeing them everywhere."
Sally
"They're incredible — I'm honestly close to getting one myself. Let me tell you about it..."
A few minutes of conversation later...
Andy
"But how do they actually ride?"
Sally
"The only way to find out is a test ride! Scan this QR to complete our safety waiver — and get 10% off accessories when you do."
2
Sign-Up — The QR Moment
📱 Andy scans Sally's QR code on his phone
Andy takes the test ride. Returns beaming.
Andy
"I love it! Do you have one in red?"
Sally
"We can order one for you — it'll be here in 2 days."
Andy
"Ok cool. I need to sleep on it. But in the meantime, what can I use my accessories discount on?"
Sally
"No worries — let me show you the toolkit, basket bag, and phone mount."
🏪 Sally earns toward her Brompton for every enrollment 👤 Andy has an incentive to buy now and is thinking about accessories 📊 Brompton captures data before any purchase
3
Sally's Win — Brompton Champion
📲 Later that day — Sally checks the Brompton Club app
Sally's test ride progress bar just crossed her monthly milestone goal.
Sally (to herself)
"11 enrollments — I hit it! That's 60% off a Brompton for me..."
Sally taps "Order My Brompton" directly in the app.
Sally
"I GOT IT! Finally ordering mine." 🎉
🚲 Sally earns a deeply discounted Brompton — becomes a true ambassador 📊 Incentive drives enrollment effort naturally across the shopfloor
4
Andy Buys Online — A Week Later
💻 brompton.com — Andy's home, Saturday morning
Andy adds an Electric C Line and accessories to his cart. His 10% member discount applies automatically.
Andy
"Oh nice — it remembered me from the shop. Let me make the most of that accessories discount!"
Order confirmed. The membership activation screen loads.
Andy
"Free flat tyre service for six months — that's exactly the reassurance I needed."
Andy activates his membership.
💻 Balfes earns showrooming commission — no channel conflict 👤 Seamless; discount honoured online 📈 10% incentive drove faster conversion at higher AOV
5
Brand Reward — Top Data Capture & Sales
One month later, 📞 Sam calls Balfes
Balfes is named a top test ride location for the spring.
Sam
"Hey Sally — your store is a top test ride location this spring. As a thank you we'd like to invite your team to join us for a gravel ride to Forty Hall Vineyard."
Sally
"Are you serious?! The team have been working so hard — they'll absolutely love this."
One month later — riding G Line bikes along the Lee Valley Pathway...
Sally
"Thanks Sam. We all love the bikes. This is brilliant."
Sam
"No sweat — you guys are the best. Let's get a glass! I can share a preview of the new X Line. Maybe we put your £2,500 account credit there?"
🍷 Deep brand relationship; first access to new product previews ❤️ Retailer becomes genuine brand advocate, not just a sales channel
6
The Flywheel Completes
🌀 Three months later — Andy is now a Promoter
Andy (to a friend)
"Honestly — best thing I've bought in years. You have to try one. Go to Balfes and ask for Sally."
Two of Andy's friends walk into Balfes the following week. Sally signs them both up for a test ride.
Sally
"Two more referrals from Andy this week. The flywheel's working." 📈
🌟 Identity reinforced; belongs to something real 🏪 Two new walk-ins, data captured for both 📈 CAC falls; referral margin rises; flywheel self-reinforces
